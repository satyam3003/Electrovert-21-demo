ESPERANZA

New Beginnings, Endless Possibilities !!


Esperanza event is exclusively designed
for first year students.


The event is planned in such a way that
participants will get to
learn, improve and grow.
  • Single Participation.


  • Entry fee is Rs. 20 per participant.


  • Event will be conducted in two rounds.


  • Second round will be conducted through webex platform.

STRUCTURE


Round 1 :

In First round, we will be conducting
a basic aptitude test covering various domains
like quantitative, logical, verbal, general knowledge and puzzels.

The test will be carried out in online mode.

Participants will be provided with all test related information beforehand.


Round 2 :


This will be surprised round.

Based on the last performance, participants
will get shortlisted for the second round.

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS


  1. There will be time limit for both the rounds
    so ensure that you complete your task within the given timing.


  2. Students clearing the first round only will be
    allowed to perform in round two.


  3. Ensure good internet connectivity throughout the event.
