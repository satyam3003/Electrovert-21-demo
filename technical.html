TECHNOTRONICS

Carve out your ideas to next level


This is a 2 day Innovative Idea Presentation Event consisting of single round.

Participants have to submit report of their idea/project and simulation before the given deadline

Each team will consist of maximum 3 members.

Event will be conducted through online platform, details of which will be conveyed to participants.

Day and Date - 5th (Saturday) & 6th (Sunday) June.

Event will be conducted in single round i.e no elimination.

Entry fee will be Rs. 40.

STRUCTURE


In this event, The participants will have a choice to take their domain of interest from given domains.
The only constraint is that basis of idea/project must be Electronics.

Participants have to submit the report and simulation of their innovative idea before the given deadline.

All participants have to present their idea in the interview.

Depending on their Idea, interview (Question and Answer session) will be conducted.

All details will be shared to participants via Whatsapp.

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS


Submissions should be in the format given by the committee.

Ensure good internet connectivity throughout the event.

Maintain the decorum during the event, any type of misbehavior is not be entertained.

Decision given by the authority will be final.
