PRESENTALK

Liberate your thoughts


The event will be paper presentation event.The event will provide participants a chance to enhance their presentation abilities.
  • Each team will be consisting of maximum 2 members.
  • Expected count of entries for the event is about 50.
  • Entry fee will be 20.
  • Event will be conducted in 2 rounds.
  • Event will be conducted through Webex Platform.
  • There will be some technical and Non-technical topics, they have to choose only one from them.

STRUCTURE


  • Round 1: (10:00am to 1:00 pm)
    • First round will be of presentation. Participants will be given presentation time of 7 minutes. Cross questioning will be from judges as well as other participants max time limit for that will 2 minutes.
  • Round 2: (3:00pm to 5:00pm)
    • In second round , teams which have shortlisted from first round will be allowed to participate in second round.

INSTRUCTIONS


  • Participants are requested to submit softcopy of abstract before a deadline on our email id.
  • Word limit for abstract is 600-700 words.
